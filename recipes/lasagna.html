Lasagna Recipe

Description:

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish with layers of pasta, savory sauce, and creamy cheese, all baked to perfection.This comforting casserole combines rich flavors and textures, making it a favorite for family meals and special occasions.

Ingredients:

For the Meat Sauce:

  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef
  • 1/2 lb (225g) Italian sausage (bulk, not in casings)
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz/800g) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 cup (240ml) water or beef broth
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional, for a bit of heat)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

For the Cheese Mixture:

  • 15 oz (425g) ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 cup (90g) grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley (or 1 tablespoon dried parsley)

For the Assembly:

  • 12 lasagna noodles (about 12 oz/340g)
  • 2 cups (200g) shredded mozzarella cheese

    • Steps:

    1. In a large skillet or Dutch oven, cook the ground beef and Italian sausage over medium heat until browned. Break it up with a spoon as it cooks.
    2. Add the chopped onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
    3. Stir in the garlic and cook for an additional 1 minute.Stir in the garlic and cook for an additional 1 minute.
    4. Add the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, and water or beef broth. Mix well.
    5. Season with basil, oregano, red pepper flakes (if using), salt, and black pepper.
    6. Bring to a simmer, then reduce the heat and let it cook uncovered for 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
    7. While the sauce is simmering, bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
    8. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions until al dente.
    9. Drain and lay the noodles out on a baking sheet or parchment paper to prevent them from sticking together.
    10. In a medium bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, egg, Parmesan cheese, and parsley. Mix until smooth.
    11. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
    12. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13-inch (23x33 cm) baking dish.
    13. Arrange a layer of lasagna noodles over the sauce.
    14. Spread a layer of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles.
    15. Sprinkle with a layer of shredded mozzarella cheese.
    16. Add a layer of meat sauce over the cheese.
    17. Repeat the layers (noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella, and meat sauce) until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of meat sauce and a generous topping of mozzarella cheese.
    18. Cover the dish with aluminum foil.
    19. Bake for 25 minutes.
    20. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 20-25 minutes, or until the top is bubbly and golden brown.
    21. Let the lasagna cool for 10-15 minutes before slicing. This helps it set and makes serving easier.

    Enjoy your delicious homemade lasagna! It’s perfect with a side salad and garlic bread.
